Bahamut!

Bahamut: King of Dragons

Bahamut is a reccuring character in the popular Final Fantasy franchise. The powerful dragon appears (or is referenced) in almost every game in the franchise. Sometimes as a boss, sometimes as an ally. Often, Bahamut will join your team after you prove your worth in battle.

List of attacks:

  • Mega Flare
  • Giga Flare
  • Terra Flare
  • Impulse
